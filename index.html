THE PROCESS

Three steps, no waiting room

Most compressors make you upload, queue, then download a link. Squeeze skips the queue entirely.

01

Drop your image

JPG, PNG, or WebP — straight from your phone, desktop, or camera roll. It loads instantly since it never has to upload.

02

Set your target

Drag the quality dial, or switch to "target size" and type the exact KB or MB you need for an email attachment, form upload, or website.

03

Download

Squeeze searches for the highest quality that fits your limit and hands you a file ready to save or share.

WHY SQUEEZE

Built for the upload limit you're staring at

Private by default

Processing happens on your device. We couldn't see your photos even if we wanted to.

Hit an exact target

Many forms cap uploads at 100KB or 1MB. Type the number and Squeeze gets you under it.

Convert formats

Export as JPG, WebP, or PNG regardless of what you started with.

No wait, no signup

No account, no email, no watermark. Open the page and start.

PNG is a lossless format, so the quality dial doesn't apply the same way. For the smallest possible file, export as JPG or WebP instead — both shrink much further.
